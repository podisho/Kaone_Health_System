Our Privacy

"Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to collecting, using, and disclosing your personal and medical information responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws."

Privacy Policy

At Kaone, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients' personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines our policies and procedures for collecting, using, and disclosing patient data.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of patient records. Our clinic/hospital is dedicated to providing a secure and trustworthy environment for our patients.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect patient information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include: - Encryption and secure servers - Access controls and authentication - Regular backups and disaster recovery plans

Patient Rights

Patients have the right to:

- Access and amend their medical records
- Request restrictions on the use and disclosure of their information
- File a complaint with our clinic/hospital or the relevant authorities

Types of Information Collected

We collect personal and medical information from our patients, including: - Contact information (name, email, phone number, etc.) - Medical history and treatment information - Billing and payment information

How We Used and Disclose Information

We use and disclose patient information for the following purposes: - Providing medical care and treatment - Billing and payment processing - Communicating with patients and healthcare providers - Quality improvement and research purposes

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify patients of any changes by posting an updated version on our website.
